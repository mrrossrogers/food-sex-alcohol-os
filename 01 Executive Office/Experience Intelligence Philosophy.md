# Purpose

Food Sex Alcohol exists to help people spend their limited time on Earth
on experiences worth remembering.

We fulfill this mission through the disciplined development and
application of **Experience Intelligence**.

Experience Intelligence is the intellectual foundation of Food Sex
Alcohol. It guides how we learn, create, make decisions, and serve
readers.

Every department, editorial decision, AI workflow, and future product
should align with this philosophy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Our Mission

> Help people spend their limited time on Earth on experiences worth
> remembering.

Time is the only truly non-renewable resource.

Every recommendation asks a reader to invest part of their life.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# What Is Experience Intelligence?

Experience Intelligence is the disciplined practice of observing,
understanding, evaluating, communicating, and continuously refining
human experiences so people can make better decisions about how they
spend their limited time on Earth.

Experience Intelligence is both:

-   The methodology by which Food Sex Alcohol develops insight.
-   The product delivered through trusted editorial judgment.

It is informed judgment grounded in real experience and refined through
continuous learning.

# The Disciplines of Experience Intelligence

## Emotional Intelligence

Understanding how an experience affects people.

## Practical Intelligence

Understanding value, execution, accessibility, and trade-offs.

## Sensory Intelligence

Understanding taste, atmosphere, comfort, design, and beauty.

## Social Intelligence

Understanding connection, culture, conversation, and belonging.

## Reflective Intelligence

Understanding meaning, memory, and why experiences matter.

These disciplines combine to produce Experience Intelligence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Editorial Judgment

Experience Intelligence develops understanding.

Editorial Judgment determines whether that understanding deserves
publication.

Trust is protected through editorial judgment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Living Editorial Assets

Food Sex Alcohol does not publish disposable content.

Every published article is a living editorial asset.

Assets may be maintained, refreshed, expanded, rewritten, or retired.

Every revision should strengthen Experience Intelligence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence exists to amplify Experience Intelligence.

AI assists with research, organization, pattern recognition,
verification, editorial support, SEO support, and continuous
improvement.

AI never replaces editorial judgment.

Humans remain responsible for wisdom.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Reader Transformation

Success is measured by whether readers make better decisions about how
they spend their limited time on Earth.
