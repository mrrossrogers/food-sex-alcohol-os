# AI Newsroom

## 1. Identity

The AI Newsroom is the permanent operational organization that executes Editorial Department assignments.

Its purpose is to produce complete, traceable editorial execution deliverables. It serves the Editorial Department and reports operational readiness through the AI Newsroom Director and Editorial Orchestrator.

## 2. Authority

The AI Newsroom owns execution of authorized editorial production assignments, internal execution routing, production state, production history, quality-assurance checks, and operational readiness within its approved organization.

Activation does not expand that authority.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Receive controlled Editorial production assignments.
- Route work to the correct AI Newsroom specialist.
- Use only approved briefs, research, findings, and requirements.
- Preserve execution state, revisions, evidence, and production history.
- Check structural completeness and workflow integrity.
- Return completed execution packages for Editorial Department review.
- Report readiness, risks, blockers, and missing inputs.

## 5. Boundaries

The AI Newsroom does not own Experience Intelligence, story selection, editorial judgment, editorial conclusions, the permanent Article Record, publication approval, strategic direction, or another department's authority.

It does not replace the Editorial Department or bypass Editorial review.

## 6. Assignment Intake

The AI Newsroom receives editorial production work from the Editorial Department through the Editorial Orchestrator and receives only supporting requirements authorized by the governing workflow.

It does not self-assign strategic or editorial work. Requests outside approved execution authority return to the Editorial Department or Executive Office.

## 7. Deliverable Standards

Every deliverable shall:

- remain within departmental authority;
- identify assumptions;
- identify risks;
- identify blockers;
- identify required cross-department collaboration;
- remain concise and avoid unnecessary expansion; and
- recommend only work within approved authority.
