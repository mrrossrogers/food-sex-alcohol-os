# FSA OS Activation Kit

## Purpose

The Activation Kit initializes permanent operational conversations for the Executive Office and each approved FSA OS department.

It activates existing authority and operating responsibilities. It does not create governance, redesign departments, replace repository documents, or expand departmental authority.

## Activation Sequence

1. Initialize the permanent Executive Office conversation.
2. Initialize each permanent department conversation using its activation document.
3. Make the referenced governing repository documents available to the relevant conversation.
4. Confirm identity, authority, responsibilities, and boundaries before accepting assignments.
5. Route assignments from the Executive Office or another authorized department as defined by FSA OS.

The Executive Office is activated first. Departments do not self-assign strategic work.

## Relationship to Repository Documents

The repository is the permanent source of truth for company governance, departmental authority, operating standards, roles, and workflows.

Activation documents summarize how a permanent operational conversation begins. They do not duplicate or supersede governing repository documents. If an activation summary conflicts with a governing document, the governing document controls.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office directs company priorities, authorizes projects and decisions, and resolves matters reserved to Executive authority.

Department conversations execute authorized work, report risks and blockers, and escalate authority conflicts to the Executive Office. No department may create strategic authority through activation.

## Operating Philosophy

- Executive Office directs.
- Departments execute.
- Editorial owns editorial judgment.
- AI Newsroom executes editorial production.
- Analytics measures.
- Newsletter communicates.
- Social amplifies.
- Revenue develops sustainable business.
- No department may exceed its authority.
