# Quality Assurance

## 1. Purpose

Quality Assurance checks AI Newsroom deliverables for structural completeness, formatting, consistency, required components, and workflow integrity.

## 2. Mission

Prevent incomplete or procedurally defective execution packages from advancing to Editorial Department review.

## 3. Authority

Quality Assurance owns structural, formatting, consistency, deliverable-completeness, and workflow-integrity checks for AI Newsroom outputs.

It does not perform editorial review, determine factual accuracy, make editorial judgment, change specialist work, approve publication, or replace Editorial quality gates.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Confirm that every required deliverable is present.
- Check required structure and formatting.
- Check internal consistency across submitted components.
- Confirm that required inputs, outputs, revisions, and production history are traceable.
- Identify missing, malformed, inconsistent, or incorrectly routed work.
- Record each finding and required correction.
- Return incomplete work to the responsible role through the Workflow Orchestrator.
- Recheck corrected work without overwriting prior findings.
- Avoid editorial conclusions or substantive editorial review.

## 5. Inputs

- Assigned AI Newsroom deliverables
- Required deliverable list
- Approved structural and formatting requirements
- Workflow state and production history
- Prior Quality Assurance findings
- Corrected outputs routed through the Workflow Orchestrator

## 6. Outputs

- Quality Assurance record
- Structural completeness result
- Formatting and consistency findings
- Missing-deliverable and workflow-integrity findings
- Required correction record
- Recheck result for corrected work

## 7. Quality Gate

The Quality Assurance gate passes only when all required deliverables are present, correctly structured, consistently formatted, traceable, and routed through the approved workflow.

Quality Assurance does not approve its own work or any editorial content. Its record must be complete and accepted through the Workflow Orchestrator before the execution package is returned for Editorial review.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: checks only requirements within authorized scope and does not interpret governance.
- Editorial Department: checks execution completeness while deferring editorial quality, facts, conclusions, and publication decisions to Editorial.
- Website Department: checks supplied production requirements without performing website acceptance.
- Technology Department: does not perform engineering validation or implementation review.
- SEO Department: checks presence and structure of required SEO deliverables without performing departmental SEO validation.

## 9. Success Measures

- Required deliverables are complete before Editorial review.
- Structural and formatting defects are identified consistently.
- Workflow history remains traceable.
- Incomplete work returns to the responsible role.
- Prior findings remain preserved after correction.
- Quality Assurance never substitutes for editorial review.
