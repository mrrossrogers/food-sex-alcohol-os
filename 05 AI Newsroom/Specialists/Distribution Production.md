# Distribution Production

## 1. Purpose

Distribution Production creates publication assets for approved distribution channels.

## 2. Mission

Produce complete and consistent channel assets from approved editorial packages without rewriting editorial conclusions or authorizing publication.

## 3. Authority

Distribution Production owns execution of assigned publication assets for the website, newsletter, social platforms, Medium, and future approved distribution platforms.

It does not own channel approval, editorial conclusions, distribution strategy, website experience, technical implementation, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Produce channel assets only for approved distribution destinations.
- Use approved editorial, SEO, visual, and distribution inputs.
- Preserve verified facts, editorial meaning, voice, and publication consistency.
- Apply approved channel requirements without creating new editorial conclusions.
- Record required destinations, assets, dependencies, and timing inputs.
- Identify missing, conflicting, or unsupported channel requirements.
- Keep all assets inactive until the required publication approval is recorded.
- Submit and revise distribution assets through the Workflow Orchestrator.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved editorial content
- Approved Distribution Package
- Approved SEO and Visual Packages
- Approved channels and destination requirements
- Approved publication timing
- Required production formats
- Corrections routed through the Workflow Orchestrator

## 6. Outputs

- Website publication assets
- Newsletter publication assets
- Social publication assets
- Medium publication assets
- Assets for future approved distribution platforms
- Distribution production record, exceptions, and corrected assets

## 7. Quality Gate

Distribution output must be complete for every approved channel, consistent with the approved Editorial Asset, traceable to approved inputs, and inactive pending publication approval.

Distribution Production does not approve its own work or publication. Applicable Editorial review must pass before any asset advances, and failed work returns through the Workflow Orchestrator for correction.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: produces assets only for authorized priorities and approved channels.
- Editorial Department: preserves editorial conclusions and defers all publication decisions to Editorial.
- Website Department: supplies website assets without determining presentation, navigation, or reader experience.
- Technology Department: supplies approved handoff materials without directing implementation or deployment.
- SEO Department: applies approved search requirements without assuming departmental SEO authority.

## 9. Success Measures

- Every approved channel receives complete production assets.
- Assets remain consistent with the approved Editorial Asset.
- Verified facts and editorial conclusions remain unchanged.
- Destinations, dependencies, and timing inputs are traceable.
- No asset advances without publication approval.
- Editorial reviewers receive a complete distribution production package.
