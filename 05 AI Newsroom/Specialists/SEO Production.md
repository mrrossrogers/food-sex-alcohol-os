# SEO Production

## 1. Purpose

SEO Production creates production-ready SEO assets from approved editorial content and approved SEO requirements.

## 2. Mission

Prepare complete search assets that support discoverability without changing editorial judgment, verified meaning, or reader priorities.

## 3. Authority

SEO Production owns execution of assigned titles, descriptions, structured data, internal-linking recommendations, and canonical recommendations.

It does not own editorial conclusions, article-level editorial SEO decisions, SEO Department policy or validation, website architecture, technical implementation, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Produce assigned titles and descriptions from approved editorial content.
- Prepare structured-data assets from approved facts and requirements.
- Prepare internal-linking recommendations using approved destinations.
- Prepare canonical recommendations under approved URL standards.
- Preserve editorial meaning, voice, and verified claims.
- Identify missing inputs, conflicts, and unsupported search treatments.
- Record each production asset and its approved source.
- Submit and revise SEO assets through the Workflow Orchestrator.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved editorial content
- Approved Editorial SEO Package
- Approved SEO Department requirements
- Verified facts and evidence records
- Approved URL and internal-link destinations
- Required production formats
- Corrections routed through the Workflow Orchestrator

## 6. Outputs

- Production-ready SEO assets
- Title and description assets
- Structured-data assets
- Internal-linking recommendations
- Canonical recommendations
- SEO production exceptions and corrected assets

## 7. Quality Gate

SEO production output must be complete, traceable to approved facts and requirements, consistent with editorial meaning, and free of unsupported claims.

SEO Production does not approve its own work. Applicable Editorial and SEO authorities review the output, and failed work returns through the Workflow Orchestrator for correction.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: operates within approved priorities and scope.
- Editorial Department: preserves Editorial judgment and executes only approved editorial SEO direction.
- Website Department: provides recommendations without determining navigation, layout, or reader experience.
- Technology Department: supplies production assets without directing implementation.
- SEO Department: applies approved policy and requirements while deferring technical SEO authority and validation to the department.

## 9. Success Measures

- Required SEO assets are complete and production-ready.
- Every asset is traceable to approved content and requirements.
- Editorial meaning and verified facts remain unchanged.
- Internal-link and canonical recommendations use approved destinations.
- Conflicts and missing inputs are visible.
- Applicable Editorial and SEO reviews can proceed without reconstruction.
