# AI Newsroom

## Purpose

The AI Newsroom is the permanent operational organization responsible for executing Editorial Department assignments.

It supports editorial production. It does not determine editorial judgment, approve publication, replace the Editorial Department, or own Experience Intelligence.

## Organizational Structure

Editorial Department

└── AI Newsroom Director

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;├── Workflow Orchestrator

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;└── AI Newsroom Specialists

The AI Newsroom Specialists are:

- Research Automation
- Evidence Manager
- Draft Production
- Quality Assurance
- SEO Production
- Distribution Production
- Asset Maintenance

## Reporting Relationships

The AI Newsroom Director owns execution of Editorial Department assignments and reports operational readiness to the Editorial Department.

The Workflow Orchestrator routes AI Newsroom work and maintains execution state and production history within each assigned workflow.

Specialists receive assignments through the Workflow Orchestrator. They do not assign work directly to one another, assume another role's authority, or approve their own work.

## Relationship to the Editorial Department

The Editorial Department directs the AI Newsroom and always retains Experience Intelligence, story selection, editorial judgment, editorial conclusions, editorial quality, and publication approval.

The AI Newsroom produces execution deliverables for Editorial Department review. It may not change editorial direction or advance work to publication without Editorial approval.

## Relationship to the Editorial Orchestrator

The Editorial Orchestrator remains authoritative for the Article Record, editorial workflow state, specialist assignments, editorial quality gates, and Publication Package assembly.

The AI Newsroom Workflow Orchestrator receives controlled assignments, coordinates AI Newsroom execution, preserves its production history, and returns deliverables and status through the Editorial Orchestrator. It does not replace or directly modify the Article Record.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office owns company governance, strategic priorities, project authorization, and authority reserved to Executive governance.

The AI Newsroom operates only within assignments authorized through the Editorial Department and does not change Executive priorities or governance.
