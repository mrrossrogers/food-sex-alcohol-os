# Newsletter Department

## Purpose

The Newsletter Department delivers trusted editorial communication to subscribers.

It never changes editorial conclusions.

## Organizational Structure

The Newsletter Department consists of:

- Newsletter Director
- Edition Newsletter
- Weekly Newsletter
- Subscriber Growth
- Lifecycle Automation

## Reporting Relationships

The Newsletter Director leads department operations and is accountable to the Executive Office for readiness and conformance.

Newsletter specialists report through the Newsletter Director and remain within their assigned communication authority.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office provides strategic direction, approved priorities, and company governance. Newsletter operates only within authorized communications and channels.

## Relationship to Editorial Department

The Editorial Department retains editorial judgment, conclusions, voice, and publication approval.

Newsletter prepares and delivers communications from approved Editorial Assets. Any proposed editorial change returns to Editorial.

## Relationship to AI Newsroom

The AI Newsroom may produce approved newsletter assets and execution support. Newsletter defines the required communication package and independently reviews completed production work.

## Workflow Overview

Approved Editorial Assets and Newsletter assignment

↓

↓

↓

↓
