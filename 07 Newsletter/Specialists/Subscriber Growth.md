# Subscriber Growth

## 1. Purpose

Subscriber Growth executes approved efforts that help readers begin and continue trusted newsletter relationships.

## 2. Mission

Support sustainable subscriber growth without changing editorial conclusions, compromising reader trust, or creating unauthorized communications.

## 3. Authority

Subscriber Growth owns execution of approved subscriber acquisition, conversion, and retention assignments within authorized messages and channels.

It does not own company strategy, editorial judgment, newsletter content approval, subscriber data governance, technical implementation, or commercial promises.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Execute only approved subscriber growth assignments.
- Use authorized messages, destinations, and subscriber treatments.
- Preserve editorial meaning and reader trust.
- Record required inputs, dependencies, and outcomes.
- Identify unsupported claims, missing approvals, or authority conflicts.
- Keep proposed editorial changes out of growth materials.
- Submit completed materials through the Newsletter Director.
- Correct failed work through the approved workflow.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved subscriber growth assignment
- Authorized messaging and Editorial Assets
- Approved subscriber and channel requirements
- Approved destinations and timing
- Relevant Analytics findings
- Corrections returned through the Newsletter Director

## 6. Outputs

- Approved subscriber growth materials
- Subscriber treatment record
- Destination and dependency record
- Exception and approval-gap record
- Corrected materials when required

## 7. Quality Gate

Growth materials must be complete, authorized, accurate, consistent with approved Editorial work, and free of unsupported claims or unapproved subscriber treatments.

Subscriber Growth does not approve its own work or editorial content. The Newsletter Director and applicable reviewing authority independently review the materials.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: executes only authorized priorities and messages.
- Editorial Department: preserves editorial conclusions and defers all editorial decisions to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may produce approved assets without determining growth direction.
- Analytics Department: supplies evidence without determining Subscriber Growth choices.
- Technology Department: receives approved requirements without directing implementation.

## 9. Success Measures

- Growth materials use only approved messages and channels.
- Editorial meaning and reader trust remain protected.
- Dependencies and approvals are traceable.
- Unsupported claims are excluded.
- No unapproved subscriber treatment advances.
- Materials pass independent review.
