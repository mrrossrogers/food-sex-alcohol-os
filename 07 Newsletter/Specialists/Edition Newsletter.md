# Edition Newsletter

## 1. Purpose

Edition Newsletter prepares subscriber communication for an approved Food Sex Alcohol Edition.

## 2. Mission

Present approved Edition content clearly and consistently without changing editorial conclusions or introducing independent editorial positions.

## 3. Authority

Edition Newsletter owns assembly and preparation of the assigned Edition newsletter within approved content, structure, and channel requirements.

It does not own Edition selection, editorial judgment, editorial conclusions, subscriber strategy, technical delivery, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Use only approved Edition assets and Newsletter assignments.
- Assemble the required Edition newsletter components.
- Preserve approved facts, conclusions, voice, links, and visual direction.
- Identify missing, conflicting, or outdated inputs.
- Keep proposed editorial changes out of production and return them to Editorial.
- Maintain consistency across newsletter components.
- Submit the completed package through the Newsletter Director.
- Correct failed work without replacing prior records.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved Edition and Editorial Assets
- Approved Newsletter assignment
- Approved SEO, visual, and distribution assets
- Authorized subscriber and channel requirements
- Required links and delivery timing
- Corrections returned through the Newsletter Director

## 6. Outputs

- Edition newsletter package
- Approved content and asset assembly
- Link and dependency record
- Missing-input and conflict record
- Corrected package when required

## 7. Quality Gate

The package must be complete, faithful to the approved Edition, consistent with channel requirements, and free of unauthorized editorial changes.

Edition Newsletter does not approve its own work or publication. The Newsletter Director and applicable Editorial authority independently review the package.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: operates within approved priorities and channels.
- Editorial Department: uses approved Edition content and defers every editorial decision to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may supply approved production assets without determining newsletter content.
- Analytics Department: receives performance findings without determining communication choices.
- Technology Department: supplies approved assets without directing delivery implementation.

## 9. Success Measures

- The Edition newsletter is complete and consistent.
- Approved editorial meaning remains intact.
- Links, assets, and dependencies are traceable.
- Missing or conflicting inputs are visible.
- No editorial conclusion is changed.
- The package passes independent review.
