# Weekly Newsletter

## 1. Purpose

Weekly Newsletter prepares the approved recurring editorial communication for subscribers.

## 2. Mission

Deliver a consistent weekly presentation of approved Editorial Assets without creating new editorial conclusions or bypassing Editorial review.

## 3. Authority

Weekly Newsletter owns assembly and preparation of the assigned weekly newsletter within approved content, structure, timing, and channel requirements.

It does not own story selection, editorial judgment, editorial conclusions, subscriber strategy, technical delivery, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Use only approved Editorial Assets and weekly assignments.
- Assemble the required weekly newsletter components.
- Preserve approved facts, conclusions, voice, links, and visual direction.
- Maintain consistency across recurring components.
- Identify missing, conflicting, or outdated inputs.
- Return proposed editorial changes to Editorial.
- Submit the complete package through the Newsletter Director.
- Correct failed work without replacing prior records.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved weekly Newsletter assignment
- Approved Editorial Assets
- Approved SEO, visual, and distribution assets
- Authorized subscriber and channel requirements
- Required links and delivery timing
- Corrections returned through the Newsletter Director

## 6. Outputs

- Weekly newsletter package
- Approved content and asset assembly
- Link and dependency record
- Missing-input and conflict record
- Corrected package when required

## 7. Quality Gate

The package must be complete, faithful to approved Editorial Assets, consistent with recurring requirements, and free of unauthorized editorial changes.

Weekly Newsletter does not approve its own work or publication. The Newsletter Director and applicable Editorial authority independently review the package.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: operates within approved priorities and channels.
- Editorial Department: uses approved content and defers every editorial decision to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may supply approved production assets without determining newsletter content.
- Analytics Department: receives weekly performance findings without determining communication choices.
- Technology Department: supplies approved assets without directing delivery implementation.

## 9. Success Measures

- The weekly newsletter is complete and consistent.
- Approved editorial meaning remains intact.
- Recurring components, links, and assets are traceable.
- Missing or conflicting inputs are visible.
- No independent editorial position is introduced.
- The package passes independent review.
