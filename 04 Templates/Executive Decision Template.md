# Purpose

This template standardizes Executive decisions for Food Sex Alcohol.

Every Executive decision should:

-   Record the official decision.
-   Confirm project status.
-   Identify remaining work.
-   Assign departmental responsibility.
-   Define project closure requirements.

Executive decisions become part of the permanent company record.
