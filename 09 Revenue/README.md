# Revenue Department

## Purpose

The Revenue Department develops sustainable revenue that supports the publication.

Revenue never influences editorial judgment. Editorial independence remains absolute.

Revenue opportunities must follow approved editorial work. Editorial shall never be modified to satisfy commercial interests.

## Organizational Structure

The Revenue Department consists of:

- Revenue Director
- Affiliate Partnerships
- Brand Partnerships
- Membership
- Future Commerce

## Reporting Relationships

The Revenue Director leads department operations and is accountable to the Executive Office for commercial readiness, integrity, and conformance.

Revenue specialists report through the Revenue Director and remain within their assigned commercial authority.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office provides strategic direction, approves revenue priorities and opportunities, and retains company governance.

Revenue executes only authorized commercial work.

## Relationship to Editorial Department

The Editorial Department retains absolute authority over Experience Intelligence, story selection, editorial judgment, conclusions, recommendations, corrections, and publication decisions.

Revenue work follows approved editorial work and may never influence or modify it.

## Relationship to AI Newsroom

The AI Newsroom may produce approved commercial-support assets after Editorial and Executive authorization. Revenue defines the required commercial package and independently reviews production output.

## Workflow Overview

Approved Editorial work

↓

Executive-authorized revenue opportunity

↓

Revenue Department execution

↓

Editorial independence and department quality review

↓

Authorized commercial activity
