# Brand Partnerships

## 1. Purpose

Brand Partnerships manages authorized commercial relationships with approved brands.

## 2. Mission

Execute transparent brand partnership obligations without influencing Editorial judgment, conclusions, recommendations, or publication decisions.

## 3. Authority

Brand Partnerships owns execution of approved brand relationships, commercial deliverables, partner records, and disclosure coordination.

It does not own editorial content, story selection, brand approval outside Executive authorization, editorial changes, reader-experience design, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Execute only Executive-authorized brand partnership assignments.
- Confirm that commercial work remains separate from Editorial judgment.
- Maintain approved partner, obligation, asset, and disclosure records.
- Use only approved commercial materials and destinations.
- Identify conflicts between partner requests and Editorial independence.
- Reject and escalate requests to modify Editorial work.
- Confirm required approvals and disclosures before activity begins.
- Submit completed records through the Revenue Director.

## 5. Inputs

- Executive-authorized brand partnership assignment
- Approved Editorial work where applicable
- Approved partner requirements and obligations
- Approved commercial assets and destinations
- Required disclosures and review status
- Corrections returned through the Revenue Director

## 6. Outputs

- Brand partnership package
- Partner and obligation record
- Approved asset and destination record
- Disclosure record
- Independence conflict and escalation record

## 7. Quality Gate

Brand partnership work must be authorized, transparent, fully disclosed, operationally complete, and free of commercial influence over Editorial work.

Brand Partnerships does not approve its own work or editorial content. The Revenue Director and applicable reviewing authority independently review the package.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: executes only authorized brand opportunities and obligations.
- Editorial Department: preserves absolute Editorial independence and routes any editorial request back to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may produce approved commercial assets without changing Editorial work.
- Website Department: supplies approved commercial requirements without directing reader experience.
- Technology Department: states approved operational needs without directing implementation.

## 9. Success Measures

- Brand activity is authorized and traceable.
- Required obligations and disclosures are complete.
- Editorial work remains unchanged.
- Commercial conflicts are rejected and escalated.
- Partner records remain complete.
- Packages pass independent review.
