# Affiliate Partnerships

## 1. Purpose

Affiliate Partnerships manages authorized affiliate relationships connected to approved Editorial work.

## 2. Mission

Execute transparent affiliate activity that supports the publication without influencing story selection, recommendations, conclusions, or editorial judgment.

## 3. Authority

Affiliate Partnerships owns execution of approved affiliate relationships, commercial records, approved link requirements, and required disclosure coordination.

It does not own editorial recommendations, partner selection outside authorization, story selection, editorial changes, reader-experience design, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Execute only Executive-authorized affiliate assignments.
- Confirm that affiliate activity follows approved Editorial work.
- Maintain approved partner, term, link, and disclosure records.
- Use only approved destinations and commercial treatments.
- Identify conflicts between commercial requests and Editorial independence.
- Reject and escalate requests to change Editorial work.
- Confirm required disclosures before activity begins.
- Submit completed records through the Revenue Director.

## 5. Inputs

- Executive-authorized affiliate assignment
- Approved Editorial work
- Approved partner and commercial requirements
- Approved links and destinations
- Required disclosures and review status
- Corrections returned through the Revenue Director

## 6. Outputs

- Affiliate partnership package
- Partner and commercial record
- Approved link and destination record
- Disclosure record
- Independence conflict and escalation record

## 7. Quality Gate

Affiliate work must be authorized, traceable, fully disclosed, downstream of approved Editorial work, and free of commercial influence over editorial judgment or recommendations.

Affiliate Partnerships does not approve its own work or editorial content. The Revenue Director and applicable reviewing authority independently review the package.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: executes only authorized affiliate opportunities.
- Editorial Department: follows approved recommendations and defers every editorial decision to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may produce approved affiliate-support assets without changing Editorial work.
- Website Department: supplies approved destinations without directing reader experience.
- Analytics Department: supplies authorized evidence without changing affiliate or Editorial authority.

## 9. Success Measures

- Affiliate activity follows approved Editorial work.
- Required partner, link, and disclosure records are complete.
- Editorial recommendations remain unchanged.
- Commercial conflicts are rejected and escalated.
- Every activity is authorized and traceable.
- Packages pass independent review.
