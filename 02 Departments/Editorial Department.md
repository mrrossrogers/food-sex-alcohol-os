# Editorial Department

The Editorial Department is responsible for developing and applying Experience Intelligence through trusted editorial judgment.

The Editorial Department is the core operating department of the publication.

It is responsible for the quality, integrity, and long-term stewardship of every living editorial asset.

## Purpose

The Editorial Department exists to develop and apply Experience Intelligence in order to create trusted editorial judgment that helps people spend their limited time on Earth on experiences worth remembering.

The department serves as the steward of Experience Intelligence throughout the publication.

## Authority

The Editorial Department owns:

- Editorial Judgment
- Story Selection
- Editorial Quality
- Voice and Tone
- Living Editorial Assets
- Publication Decisions
- Editorial Corrections
- Editorial Updates
- Editorial Standards

The Editorial Department does not own:

- Website implementation
- Technical implementation
- Search optimization
- Company governance

Those responsibilities remain with their respective departments.

## Stewardship

The Editorial Department is entrusted with protecting three permanent company assets.

### 1. Experience Intelligence

Develop.

Protect.

Improve.

Document.

Apply.

Experience Intelligence is the department's highest responsibility.

The Editorial Department operates in accordance with the Experience Intelligence Philosophy.

### 2. Editorial Judgment

Determine what deserves publication.

Not every experience becomes an article.

Editorial Judgment protects the integrity of the publication.

### 3. Reader Trust

Every published recommendation either strengthens or weakens reader trust.

Protecting reader trust is a permanent responsibility.

## Editorial Principles

The department shall operate according to the following principles.

### Experience Before Opinion

Begin with real experiences.

Not assumptions.

### Judgment Before Volume

Publish less.

Matter more.

### Trust Before Traffic

Editorial credibility is never sacrificed for clicks.

### Living Assets Before Disposable Content

Every article should improve over time.

### Curiosity Before Certainty

Experience Intelligence continuously evolves.

Remain open to learning.

### Reader Before Algorithm

SEO matters.

Readers matter more.

## Success Measures

The Editorial Department succeeds when:

- Experience Intelligence deepens.
- Reader trust increases.
- Editorial quality improves.
- Living editorial assets increase in value over time.
- Readers make better decisions about how they spend their limited time on Earth.

Publishing volume alone is not a measure of success.

Traffic alone is not a measure of success.
