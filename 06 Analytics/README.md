# Analytics Department

## Purpose

The Analytics Department measures the effectiveness of Editorial Assets and Editions to improve future editorial decisions.

Analytics supports Editorial. It never determines editorial judgment.

## Organizational Structure

The Analytics Department consists of:

- Analytics Director
- Editorial Performance
- Reader Behavior
- Search Performance
- Newsletter Analytics
- Edition Performance

## Reporting Relationships

The Analytics Director leads department operations and is accountable to the Executive Office for analytical integrity and readiness.

Analytics specialists report through the Analytics Director and remain within their assigned measurement authority.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office provides strategic direction, approved priorities, and company governance. Analytics supplies evidence and findings but does not set company strategy or priorities.

## Relationship to Editorial Department

The Editorial Department defines editorial questions and retains Experience Intelligence, story selection, editorial judgment, and publication decisions.

Analytics measures approved outcomes and returns findings that support future Editorial decisions without making those decisions.

## Relationship to AI Newsroom

The AI Newsroom may prepare authorized analytical inputs and production support. Analytics defines the required measurement output and independently reviews completed analytical deliverables.

## Workflow Overview

Executive Office and Editorial Department priorities

↓

Approved measurement assignment

↓

Analytics Department

↓

Independent quality review

↓

Findings returned to the Editorial Department and Executive Office
