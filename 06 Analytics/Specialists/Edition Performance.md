# Edition Performance

## 1. Purpose

Edition Performance measures the combined effectiveness of approved Food Sex Alcohol Editions.

## 2. Mission

Provide complete Edition-level findings that help Editorial understand outcomes across related Editorial Assets without determining future editorial choices.

## 3. Authority

Edition Performance owns measurement, comparison, and reporting of authorized Edition-level performance data within assigned scope.

It does not own Edition selection, editorial judgment, asset evaluation, publication decisions, distribution direction, or strategic priorities.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Measure assigned Edition outcomes.
- Consolidate authorized findings across Edition components.
- Compare approved Editions and periods.
- Identify material trends, relationships, and anomalies.
- Preserve distinctions between component results and Edition-level findings.
- Document scope, uncertainty, and limitations.
- Maintain traceability to each measured Edition and Editorial Asset.
- Return findings through the Analytics Director.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved measurement assignment
- Authorized Edition records
- Authorized component performance data
- Approved comparison periods and standards
- Prior findings where relevant
- Corrections returned through the Analytics Director

## 6. Outputs

- Edition performance report
- Consolidated component findings
- Edition comparison and trend record
- Scope, uncertainty, and limitation record
- Corrected report when required

## 7. Quality Gate

The report must be complete, traceable to approved Editions and component inputs, internally consistent, and explicit about limitations.

Edition Performance does not approve its own work or determine future Editorial choices. The Analytics Director independently reviews the report.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: operates within approved priorities.
- Editorial Department: supplies Edition evidence while deferring all editorial judgment and asset decisions to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may support approved consolidation without determining findings.
- Newsletter and Social Departments: incorporates authorized channel outcomes without directing their operations.
- Website Department: incorporates authorized experience outcomes without determining reader experience.

## 9. Success Measures

- Edition findings are complete and traceable.
- Component and Edition-level results remain distinguishable.
- Comparisons use approved scope and periods.
- Trends and limitations are visible.
- Findings support, but do not determine, Editorial decisions.
- Reports pass independent review.
