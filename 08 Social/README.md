# Social Department

## Purpose

The Social Department extends the reach of approved Editorial Assets.

Social never creates independent editorial positions. Social amplifies approved editorial.

## Organizational Structure

The Social Department consists of:

- Social Director
- Editorial Promotion
- Community Engagement
- Platform Publishing
- Performance Review

## Reporting Relationships

The Social Director leads department operations and is accountable to the Executive Office for readiness and conformance.

Social specialists report through the Social Director and remain within their assigned promotion, engagement, publishing, or review authority.

## Relationship to Executive Office

The Executive Office provides strategic direction, approved priorities, and company governance. Social operates only within authorized platforms and assignments.

## Relationship to Editorial Department

The Editorial Department retains story selection, editorial judgment, conclusions, voice, and publication approval.

Social amplifies approved Editorial Assets and returns any proposed editorial change, correction, or new position to Editorial.

## Relationship to AI Newsroom

The AI Newsroom may produce approved social and distribution assets. Social defines the required platform package and independently reviews completed production work.

## Workflow Overview

Approved Editorial Asset and Social assignment

↓

Social Department production

↓

Editorial review and department quality gate

↓

Authorized platform publishing and engagement

↓

Performance findings returned through Analytics
