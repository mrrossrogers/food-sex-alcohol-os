# Editorial Promotion

## 1. Purpose

Editorial Promotion prepares social treatments that amplify approved Editorial Assets.

## 2. Mission

Extend editorial reach with accurate and compelling promotion that preserves approved meaning, voice, and conclusions.

## 3. Authority

Editorial Promotion owns preparation of assigned promotional copy, asset selection, link treatments, and approved platform adaptations.

It does not own editorial judgment, new editorial positions, story selection, platform publishing, community responses, performance analysis, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Use only approved Editorial Assets and Social assignments.
- Prepare platform-appropriate promotional treatments.
- Preserve verified facts, editorial conclusions, voice, and visual direction.
- Use approved links, assets, and calls to action.
- Identify missing, conflicting, or unsupported inputs.
- Return proposed editorial changes to Editorial.
- Submit completed treatments through the Social Director.
- Correct failed work without replacing prior records.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved Editorial Asset
- Approved Social assignment
- Approved SEO, visual, and distribution assets
- Approved platform requirements
- Authorized links and timing
- Corrections returned through the Social Director

## 6. Outputs

- Editorial promotion package
- Platform-specific promotional treatments
- Approved link and asset record
- Missing-input and conflict record
- Corrected package when required

## 7. Quality Gate

Promotional treatments must be complete, faithful to the approved Editorial Asset, appropriate for the approved platform, and free of new editorial positions or unsupported claims.

Editorial Promotion does not approve its own work or publication. The Social Director and applicable Editorial authority independently review the package.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: operates within approved priorities and platforms.
- Editorial Department: amplifies approved work and defers every editorial decision to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may supply approved production assets without determining promotion content.
- Analytics Department: supplies performance evidence without directing editorial treatments.
- Website Department: uses approved destinations without changing navigation or reader experience.

## 9. Success Measures

- Promotional treatments accurately represent approved Editorial Assets.
- Editorial meaning, voice, and conclusions remain intact.
- Links and assets are complete and traceable.
- Unsupported claims and new positions are excluded.
- Platform requirements are satisfied.
- The package passes independent review.
