# Platform Publishing

## 1. Purpose

Platform Publishing executes authorized publication of approved social assets.

## 2. Mission

Publish complete and consistent social packages to approved platforms without changing content, timing authority, or editorial conclusions.

## 3. Authority

Platform Publishing owns execution of approved social posts, asset placement, links, scheduling instructions, and publishing records for authorized platforms.

It does not own platform strategy, editorial judgment, content approval, community engagement, technical implementation, or publication authorization.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Publish only approved social packages to authorized platforms.
- Follow approved assets, links, timing, and placement requirements.
- Preserve editorial copy, conclusions, and visual direction.
- Confirm required approval before publishing.
- Identify missing assets, broken destinations, or conflicting instructions.
- Pause work when approval or required inputs are absent.
- Maintain a complete publishing record.
- Route corrections through the Social Director.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved social package
- Recorded Editorial and Social approval
- Authorized platforms and destinations
- Approved assets, links, timing, and placement requirements
- Required publishing record fields
- Corrections returned through the Social Director

## 6. Outputs

- Published social assets
- Platform publishing record
- Destination and asset confirmation
- Publishing exception record
- Corrected publishing record when required

## 7. Quality Gate

Publishing may proceed only when the social package, platform, assets, links, timing, and required approvals are complete and authorized.

Platform Publishing does not approve its own work, content, or publication. Missing approval or inconsistent inputs pause the workflow and return through the Social Director.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: publishes only to authorized platforms within approved priorities.
- Editorial Department: uses approved content and recorded publication decisions.
- AI Newsroom: may supply approved platform assets without authorizing publishing.
- Analytics Department: receives publishing records for approved measurement.
- Technology Department: reports technical publishing issues without directing implementation.

## 9. Success Measures

- Only approved packages are published.
- Assets, links, timing, and destinations are accurate.
- Editorial content remains unchanged.
- Publishing records are complete and traceable.
- Missing approval always pauses work.
- Publishing execution passes independent review.
