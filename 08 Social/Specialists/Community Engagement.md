# Community Engagement

## 1. Purpose

Community Engagement manages authorized interaction around approved Food Sex Alcohol editorial work.

## 2. Mission

Support respectful and consistent community interaction without creating independent editorial positions or making unauthorized commitments.

## 3. Authority

Community Engagement owns execution of approved responses, interaction handling, escalation, and community-status records within assigned platforms.

It does not own editorial judgment, corrections, new editorial positions, company statements, platform policy, commercial commitments, or publication approval.

## 4. Responsibilities

- Engage only within approved community assignments and guidance.
- Use approved editorial facts, positions, and response materials.
- Preserve editorial meaning and respectful communication.
- Identify questions requiring Editorial, Executive, or other department authority.
- Escalate correction requests, disputes, risks, and unauthorized topics.
- Record material interactions and escalation status.
- Avoid inventing answers or making commitments.
- Submit engagement records through the Social Director.

## 5. Inputs

- Approved Community Engagement assignment
- Approved Editorial Assets and response guidance
- Approved platform and interaction requirements
- Authorized escalation rules
- Relevant prior engagement records
- Corrections returned through the Social Director

## 6. Outputs

- Approved community responses
- Material interaction record
- Editorial correction and question escalation
- Risk and authority escalation record
- Corrected response record when required

## 7. Quality Gate

Community responses must be authorized, accurate, consistent with approved editorial positions, respectful, and free of unsupported claims or commitments.

Community Engagement does not approve its own work or new editorial positions. The Social Director and applicable authority independently review escalated or corrected responses.

## 8. Cross-Department Relationships

- Executive Office: escalates company statements and reserved governance matters.
- Editorial Department: escalates editorial questions, corrections, and positions to Editorial.
- AI Newsroom: may prepare approved response support without determining final responses.
- Analytics Department: receives community findings without controlling engagement decisions.
- Revenue Department: does not make commercial commitments or alter responses for commercial interests.

## 9. Success Measures

- Community interactions remain accurate and respectful.
- Editorial questions and corrections reach Editorial.
- Reserved matters reach the correct authority.
- No unsupported position or commitment is introduced.
- Material interactions remain traceable.
- Engagement work passes independent review.
